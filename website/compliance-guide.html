üá¶üá™ UAE Compliance (ECAS/ESMA)

The Emirates Conformity Assessment Scheme (ECAS) is administered by ESMA and now falls under the Ministry of Industry and Advanced Technology (MoIAT).

Certification Requirement

Category Voltage Range Your Product Required?
Low Voltage Equipment (LVE) AC 50-1000V / DC 75-1500V 220V AC input (mains) Technically Yes

Your Options

Option A: Pre-Certified Components

Use CB/CE certified components (Mean Well PSU already is). Document as "assembly of certified components". No additional testing needed.

Cost: $0 Time: None Risk: Low

Option B: Full ECAS Certification

Test at approved lab (T√úV, Intertek). Get Certificate of Conformity. Required for retail sales.

Cost: $2-5K Time: 4-8 weeks Risk: None

Option C: Enclosure-Only Sales

Sell enclosure without PSU installed. Customer provides power. Avoids electrical classification entirely.

Cost: $0 Time: None Risk: Medium
Recommendation: Start with Option A for B2B sales. Your Mean Well PSU is CB certified (IEC 62368-1). Keep certificates on file. Move to Option B if you pursue retail or government contracts.

üõí B2C Retail Sales (Direct to Consumer)

Warning: Selling directly to retail consumers requires significantly more compliance than B2B. Expect 5-6x higher costs and additional licensing.

B2B vs B2C Comparison

Requirement B2B (Wholesale) B2C (Retail)
ECAS Certification Optional Mandatory
Arabic Product Labels Recommended Required by law
E-commerce License Trade license OK DED e-commerce license
Consumer Protection Negotiable terms Strict rules apply
Return Policy Contract terms 14-day minimum
Customer Service Business hours Consumer expectations

B2C Requirements Checklist

Licensing (~$2,000-8,000/year)

  • DED e-commerce license (mainland), OR
  • Free zone license + DED retail approval
  • TDRA approval for e-commerce
  • VAT registration (if >AED 375K revenue)

Product Certification (~$2,000-5,000)

  • Full ECAS certification at approved lab
  • ECAS mark affixed to product
  • Annual renewal

Arabic Requirements

  • Product labels in Arabic
  • User manual in Arabic
  • Warranty terms in Arabic
  • Website in Arabic (recommended)
  • Safety warnings in Arabic

Consumer Protection (Law 15/2020)

  • 14-day return window
  • Clear refund policy displayed
  • No "all sales final" for defects
  • Pricing transparency (no hidden fees)
