1. The Problem We're Solving

Why Standard Deck Boxes Fail in the Gulf

  • Plastic degrades - UV and 50¬∞C+ heat break down resin boxes within 2-3 years
  • Not truly sealed - Intentional ventilation gaps let in humid, salty air
  • No monitoring - You don't know there's a problem until cushions are moldy
  • Heat buildup - Dark plastic boxes can reach 70¬∞C+ inside, damaging foam and fabrics
  • Corrosion - Metal hinges and latches rust within months near pools

The Science: Why Humidity Control Matters

Safe humidity range 30-50% RH - Below this, materials dry out; above, mold grows
Mold growth threshold >60% RH - Mold spores activate and colonize fabrics
Dubai average humidity 60-90% RH (higher near coast and pools)
Temperature effect Every 10¬∞C rise doubles mold growth rate

7. Usage Best Practices

‚òÄÔ∏è

Dry Before Storing

Always ensure cushions are completely dry before storing. Even sealed storage can't remove moisture that's already in the fabric.

üîÑ

Check Desiccant Monthly

Look at the indicator window. If pink/saturated, regenerate (microwave 2-3 mins) or replace the silica gel.

üì±

Set Alerts at 55% RH

This gives you time to act before mold conditions (60%+) develop. Check and refresh desiccant if alerts trigger frequently.

üåô

Ideal Storage Position

Place in shade or partial shade if possible. North-facing walls are best. Avoid direct afternoon sun exposure.

‚ö†Ô∏è Not Suitable For

Food storage, flammable materials, or electronics. This enclosure is designed for soft goods (cushions, towels, pool toys) only. For pool chemicals, use dedicated chemical storage.

9. Research & Development Notes

Key Findings from Market Research

  • Standard deck boxes use intentional ventilation to prevent condensation in temperate climates, but this allows humid Gulf air inside
  • Humidity above 60% enables mold growth; Gulf ambient humidity is typically 60-90%
  • IoT sensors (SensorPush, UbiBot, SwitchBot) provide affordable monitoring with 12+ month battery life
  • IP65/IP66 industrial enclosures demonstrate that proper gasket sealing is achievable
  • Silica gel desiccants are reusable (regenerate in microwave) and effective in enclosed spaces
  • No current market offering combines sealed construction + smart monitoring + active humidity control
