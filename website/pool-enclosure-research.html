The Sizing Problem

Why TV Enclosures Work
  • Standardized sizes: 55", 65", 75", 85"
  • VESA mounts: Universal mounting pattern
  • Predictable depth: All TVs ~50-80mm deep
  • Result: 3-4 SKUs cover 90% of market
‚úÖ
Easy to productize - Standard sizes, stock inventory, ship anywhere
Why Pool Enclosures Are Harder
  • No standard sizes: Every equipment pad is different
  • Mixed equipment: Pump + filter + chlorinator + ???
  • Plumbing varies: Pipes enter from different directions
  • Space constraints: Wall proximity, access needs
‚ùå
Hard to productize - Custom sizing, site visits, bespoke builds

Pool Chiller Enclosure Opportunity

The Problem

  • Chillers are LARGE (100√ó50√ó80cm typical)
  • Chillers are LOUD (compressor noise)
  • Chillers are UGLY (industrial units)
  • Chillers need AIRFLOW (can't fully enclose)

The Solution

  • Louvered screen enclosure (not sealed box)
  • Acoustic baffles on intake/exhaust
  • Architectural finish (matches villa aesthetic)
  • Partial noise reduction (50-60% vs 80%)
