1. Scope & Design Priorities

Target Market: Luxury residential and premium hospitality in UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Kuwait. Must accommodate standard consumer TVs while maintaining reliability in extreme conditions.

Design Priorities (Ranked)

  1. Thermal reliability in 55°C ambient + solar load
  2. PWM-controlled fans (variable speed, low-speed idle mode)
  3. Dust/sand ingress resistance (filtered intake with MERV 8)
  4. Corrosion resistance (coastal UAE specification)
  5. Premium fit/finish (luxury market positioning)
  6. Ducted thermal design (160mm depth with rear-plenum + diffuser)
Non-Negotiable Requirements:
  • NO on/off thermostats - PWM control only
  • NO unfiltered intakes - automotive-grade filtration required
  • NO consumer power strips - proper power distribution only

2. Environmental Requirements

Temperature

  • Operating: 0°C to 55°C continuous
  • Storage: -20°C to 70°C
  • Internal target: <45°C at 55°C ambient

Moisture & Humidity

  • Humidity: up to 95% RH non-condensing
  • Rain: heavy/wind-driven (not submersible)
  • Optional: ePTFE breather membrane

Dust & Sand

  • Designed for sandstorms
  • MERV 8 gasketed filter drawer
  • IP55 labyrinth baffles at intake

Corrosion

  • Salt fog: >500 hours
  • UV stability: 5+ years
  • 316 stainless hardware

IP Rating Target Target

IP55 target (validation pending) - Designed for dust protection + low-pressure water resistance. Suitable for rain, splashes, and general outdoor exposure.

Important - Ventilated Design:

This is an actively ventilated enclosure. While designed to IP55 standard, direct high-pressure water jets at intake/exhaust openings should be avoided. The labyrinth baffle system protects against rain and normal cleaning, but is not rated for pressure washer use.

IP55 Design Features:
  • First digit (5): Dust protected - limited ingress, not harmful to operation
  • Second digit (5): Protected against low-pressure water jets from any direction (6.3mm nozzle, 12.5 L/min)
  • Achieved through EPDM gaskets, 80mm integrated bezels with recessed ventilation slots, and gasketed filter drawer
  • Clean design: No protruding deflector - flush front face with recessed slots
  • Validation: IP55 rating to be confirmed via prototype testing per IEC 60529

4.8 Mounting System (Two Separate Systems)

Key Concept: There are TWO independent mounting systems:
  1. Internal: TV attaches to vertical rails inside the enclosure
  2. External: Enclosure attaches to wall via rear VESA plate
These systems are completely separate and serve different purposes.

A. Internal TV Mount (Vertical Rail System)

The TV mounts inside the enclosure using two vertical rails with adjustable TV brackets. The brackets bolt to the rails, then connect to the TV's own VESA holes.

How it works: Rails → TV Brackets → TV's VESA holes
The TV has standard VESA holes on its back. The brackets connect these holes to the vertical rails.

Vertical Mounting Rails (×2)

  • Orientation: Vertical (running top-to-bottom inside enclosure)
  • Material: 3mm steel, powder coated black
  • Length: 800mm (allows full height adjustment)
  • Width: 40mm C-channel profile
  • Hole pattern: M6 threaded holes every 25mm along length
  • Spacing: Rails positioned 400mm or 600mm apart (to match TV's VESA width)

TV Mounting Brackets (×2)

  • Type: L-shaped or hook-style brackets
  • Rail side: Bolts to rail using M6 holes (pick any height)
  • TV side: Bolts to TV's VESA holes (M6 or M8 depending on TV)
  • Adjustment: Slide up/down rails before tightening

Rail End Brackets (×4)

  • Material: 3mm steel, powder coated
  • Function: Mount rails to enclosure frame
  • Adjustment: Horizontal slotted holes (±30mm) for width adjustment
  • Hardware: M6 bolts with lock washers
  • TV VESA Compatibility: 200×200, 300×300, 400×400, 400×600, 600×400mm (standard TV patterns)
  • Height adjustment: Holes every 25mm allow precise TV centering behind glass
  • Hardware kit: M6×15mm bolts (×8), M8×20mm bolts (×4), spacers (15/20/25mm), lock washers
  • Load rating: 75kg maximum (3× safety factor for 25kg TV)

B. External Wall Mount (Enclosure to Wall)

The entire enclosure (with TV inside) mounts to the wall. A steel VESA plate is welded to the rear panel - an articulating wall bracket bolts to this plate.

How it works: Wall → Articulating Bracket → Rear Panel VESA Plate → Enclosure
This is separate from the internal TV rails. The rear plate holds the whole box on the wall.

Rear Panel VESA Plate

  • Pattern: 400×400 and 600×400mm holes
  • Material: 6mm steel reinforcement plate
  • Hardware: M8 threaded inserts
  • Function: Articulating wall bracket attaches here

Articulating Wall Bracket (Included)

  • Type: Full-motion dual-arm mount
  • Extension: Up to 500mm from wall
  • Swivel: ±90° horizontal
  • Tilt: +5° / -15°
  • Rating: 75kg capacity minimum
  • Wall plate: Heavy-duty steel with 316 SS hardware for masonry/stud mounting
  • Cable routing: Arm includes cable management channel
  • Recess installation: Bracket mounts toward TOP of enclosure to prevent tilt

5. Front Glass / Optical Stack Premium

Key Differentiator: Laminated glass with IR rejection reduces solar heat gain by up to 40%, significantly reducing cooling requirements.

5.1 Glass Construction (6mm Laminated - Locked Spec)

Layer Specification
Outer pane 3mm low-iron tempered glass
Interlayer 0.76mm PVB (polyvinyl butyral) - shatter retention
Inner pane 3mm low-iron tempered glass
Total thickness ~6.76mm laminated (3+3)
6mm Glass Specification Justification:
  • Weight reduction: ~7kg savings vs 8mm (4+4), reducing total enclosure weight to ~38kg
  • Structural adequacy: 6mm laminated meets EN 12150-1 for safety glass; continuous channel retention distributes load across full perimeter
  • Industry precedent: Competitors (Apollo, TV Shield) use 4-6mm glass successfully
  • Deflection: Maximum mid-span deflection <3mm under 60 km/h wind load (acceptable for framed glass)
  • Impact resistance: Laminated construction retains fragments if broken; PVB interlayer provides shatter resistance

Note: 8mm (4+4) available as upgrade option for high-impact environments (+$50).

5.2 Coatings & Films

Coating Purpose Performance
AR Coating (both sides) Reduce reflections <1% reflection
IR Rejection Film Block infrared heat 90% IR rejection (3M Prestige 90 or equiv)
UV Block Protect TV from UV ≥99% UV rejection

5.3 Optical Requirements

  • Haze: low - no distortion, no waves
  • Edge finish: polished
  • Glass retention: mechanical frame + gasket (not adhesive-only)

6.4 Control System New

NO on/off thermostats. PWM microcontroller control is mandatory.

Controller

  • MCU-based PWM fan controller
  • Smooth fan ramp (no sudden start)
  • Pre-cool option (user selectable)
  • Post-run cooldown after TV power-off

Sensors

  • Internal air temp (NTC/DS18B20)
  • Exhaust air temp
  • Optional: TV rear panel probe
  • Optional: Ambient intake sensor

Failsafe Required

  • If internal >65°C for >5 min:
  • → Alarm output trigger
  • → Full fan speed
  • → Optional: TV power cut

  • Spares: 1 extra filter included per unit

6.6 IP55 Integrated Bezel Ventilation (v5.3.0) IP55

Clean Design - No Deflector: v5.3.0 eliminates the protruding exhaust deflector. Ventilation is integrated into wider 80mm top/bottom bezels with recessed slots. This creates a flush "picture frame" appearance while maintaining IP55 protection.

Bottom Bezel - 80mm Integrated Intake

  • Bezel height: 80mm (wider than 40mm side bezels)
  • Slots: Multiple horizontal slots (3-4mm × 1600mm) recessed 30-40mm
  • Protection: Bezel edge overhangs slots, prevents direct water entry
  • Filter: MERV 8 standard, gasketed drawer for IP55 seal
  • Service door: 1650 × 80mm hinged panel, 4 latches
  • Mesh: 316 SS insect screen behind slots

Top Bezel - 80mm Integrated Exhaust

  • Bezel height: 80mm (wider than 40mm side bezels)
  • Slots: Multiple horizontal slots (3-4mm × 1600mm) recessed 30-40mm
  • Protection: Bezel edge overhangs slots, shields from rain
  • Fans: 3× Delta AFB1412HH-A (140mm PWM)
  • NO deflector: Flush front face, clean aesthetic
  • Mesh: 316 SS insect screen behind slots
  • Thermal: 165-195 CFM adequate for Gulf climate
Design Benefits (v5.3.0):
  • Premium aesthetic: Flush "picture frame" appearance, no industrial look
  • Simpler fabrication: No separate deflector piece to manufacture
  • Symmetric profile: Top and bottom bezels match (both 80mm)
  • Still IP55: Recessed slots protected by bezel overhang
