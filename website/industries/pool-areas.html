Common Questions

Can I use my existing TV?

Yes! Any standard flat-screen TV up to the enclosure's size limit will work. Samsung, LG, Sony, TCL - all work perfectly. You keep all your smart TV features and use your normal remote.

Will it survive Dubai summer?

Yes. We're rated for 55¬∞C ambient temperature - hotter than any Dubai summer day. The active cooling system keeps your TV at safe operating temperature even in direct afternoon sun.

Can I install an Apple TV or streaming box inside?

Yes! There's a 4-socket internal power strip. Put your Apple TV, OSN box, or gaming console inside the enclosure. Connect via short HDMI cable to the TV. Everything stays protected.

Is it loud?

No. Our PWM-controlled fans run quietly at low speed most of the time. They only speed up when internal temperature rises. Much quieter than competitors with simple on/off thermostats.

What if my TV breaks?

Simply open the enclosure and swap the TV. No need to buy a whole new unit like with "outdoor TVs". Replace with any TV of the same size. Local purchase, local warranty.

Do you install?

We can recommend installation partners, or your own contractor/handyman can install. It mounts like a standard wall bracket - any electrician or AV installer can handle it.
