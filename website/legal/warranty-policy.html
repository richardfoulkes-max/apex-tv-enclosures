2-Year Manufacturer's Warranty
All Apex Enclosures products are backed by a comprehensive 2-year warranty against defects in materials and workmanship.

1. Warranty Coverage

1.1 Duration

This warranty is valid for 24 months (2 years) from the date of delivery to the original purchaser.

1.2 What Is Covered

Under normal use conditions, this warranty covers:

Structural defects in the aluminum enclosure frame
Defects in welding or joint integrity
Manufacturing defects in the tempered glass panel
Failure of factory-installed cooling fans within warranty period
Defects in door hinges, gas struts, and locking mechanisms
Failure of the thermal management control system
Defects in gaskets and weather sealing (IP66 rated)
Premature failure of powder coating under normal conditions
Electrical component failure (thermostat, controller)

1.3 What Is NOT Covered

This warranty does not cover:

Damage resulting from improper installation
Damage from misuse, abuse, neglect, or accidents
Damage from unauthorized modifications or repairs
Normal wear and tear (scratches, fading from UV exposure)
Damage from extreme weather events (hurricanes, floods, sandstorms)
Damage from vandalism, theft, or intentional damage
Damage from power surges or improper electrical supply
Consumable items (air filters, if applicable)
The television or any third-party equipment installed in the enclosure
Cosmetic damage that does not affect functionality
Products purchased from unauthorized resellers

Critical Notice: Apex Enclosures protects your TV from weather. We do NOT warrant the TV itself. Please read this section carefully.

  • Cooling system operation

  • The TV (any brand, any issue)
  • TV overheating damage
  • TV manufacturer warranty claims
  • TV brightness or image quality
  • Soundbars, streaming devices, cables

2.2 TV Compatibility & Risk Acknowledgment

By installing a TV in an Apex Enclosure, you acknowledge:

  1. Operating a TV outdoors voids most TV warranties — this is standard industry practice regardless of enclosure brand
  2. We design for thermal protection, not guarantee it — our thermal system is engineered for Gulf conditions (55°C ambient + solar load), but actual performance depends on TV model, installation location, and usage patterns
  3. TV selection matters — not all TVs are suitable. OLED panels, plasma TVs, and models without adequate heat dissipation may fail despite enclosure protection
  4. You must follow our TV compatibility guidelines — installing incompatible TVs voids any claim related to thermal damage

2.3 What Happens If Your TV Overheats?

Our Position: If your TV fails due to heat, we will inspect the enclosure to verify our thermal system was operating correctly. If the enclosure performed as designed, TV replacement is the owner's responsibility.

We will cover:

  • Fan replacement if fans were defective
  • Controller repair if thermal control system malfunctioned
  • Airflow investigation if design defect caused inadequate cooling

We will NOT cover:

  • TV replacement or repair under any circumstances
  • Claims where incompatible TV was installed
  • Overheating caused by blocked vents or poor installation
  • TVs operated beyond recommended ambient temperatures

2.4 Recommendations to Protect Yourself

  • Consider TV insurance — some home insurance policies cover outdoor electronics
  • Use recommended TV brands — see our TV Compatibility Guide for heat-tolerant models
  • Professional installation — proper mounting and airflow clearances are critical
  • Monitor temperatures — our enclosures show fan status; consider a smart plug with energy monitoring to detect abnormal power consumption
