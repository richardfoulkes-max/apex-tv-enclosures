Request for Quotation: 75" Outdoor TV Enclosure

Australian Manufacturing Partner Inquiry

Product Overview

Premium weatherproof enclosure for protecting commercial-grade TVs in outdoor environments. Designed for the Australian market including poolside installations, outdoor entertaining areas, patios, and hospitality venues.

Key Specifications

External Dimensions 1760 × 1120 × 160mm
Primary Material 5052-H32 Aluminum (marine grade)
Sheet Thickness 2.0mm body panels / 3.0mm structural components
Finish Powder coat, exterior grade (1000hr+ salt spray)
IP Rating IP55 (jet resistant, hose-cleanable)
Thermal System Forced-air cooling, 3× PWM fans, 165-195 CFM
Front Panel 6mm laminated safety glass (3+3), anti-reflective optional
Access Bottom service door with compression latches

Manufacturing Scope

  • CNC sheet metal fabrication (laser cut, press brake, welding)
  • Powder coating to exterior grade specification
  • EPDM gasket installation for IP55 sealing
  • Assembly of thermal management system (fans, filters, controller)
  • Glass panel installation with continuous channel retention
  • Quality inspection per supplied acceptance criteria
Note: We supply complete fabrication drawings (DXF/STEP), BOM with all component specifications, assembly instructions, and quality acceptance criteria. Electronics (fans, controller, sensors) can be supplier-sourced or we can provide.
