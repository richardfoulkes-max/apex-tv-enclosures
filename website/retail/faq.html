The Basics

What is an outdoor TV enclosure?

An outdoor TV enclosure is a weatherproof housing that allows you to use a standard indoor TV in outdoor locations. Instead of buying an expensive "outdoor TV", you put your regular TV inside our protective enclosure.

Key benefit: Use any TV you want, upgrade it anytime, and keep your TV's local warranty. A 75" Samsung costs around AED 3,500 - put it in our AED 10,500 enclosure and you have outdoor entertainment for AED 14,000 vs AED 40,000+ for a purpose-built outdoor TV.
How is this different from an outdoor TV?

Outdoor TVs (like Samsung Terrace or SunBrite) are specialized televisions with weatherproofing built-in. They cost 3-5x more than regular TVs, have limited model choices, and when they break, you buy a whole new TV.

Our enclosure approach:

  • Use any TV brand/model you prefer
  • Upgrade the TV anytime without replacing the enclosure
  • If the TV breaks, swap it locally with warranty support
  • Total cost is typically 40-60% less than outdoor TVs
How does the cooling work?

We use a ducted rear-plenum cooling system with three 140mm high-static pressure fans. Cool air enters through filtered intakes at the bottom, flows across the back of the TV (where heat sinks are), and exits through the top exhaust.

PWM smart control: Fans run at low speed when cool, automatically increase when temperature rises. This means quiet operation most of the time, with full cooling power available when needed in peak summer heat.

TV Compatibility

What TV brands work?

Any standard LED or QLED flat-screen TV will work: Samsung, LG, Sony, TCL, Hisense - all are compatible. The TV just needs standard VESA mounting holes on the back.

For outdoor use, look for LED, QLED, or QNED technology - these offer good brightness for daylight viewing and don't suffer from burn-in.

Avoid OLED TVs: OLED panels are not suitable for outdoor use. They're not bright enough for daylight viewing, can suffer burn-in from static content (sports scores, news tickers), and the organic compounds degrade faster in heat. Stick with LED, QLED, or QNED technology.

Can I use a curved TV?

No, the enclosure is designed for flat-panel TVs only. Curved TVs have different depth profiles that don't fit our universal mounting system.

Do I keep my TV remote and smart features?

Yes! The glass front is IR-transparent, so your standard TV remote works normally. All smart TV features (Netflix, YouTube, etc.) work exactly as they do indoors.

Weather Protection

Will it survive Dubai summer?

Yes. We're rated for 55°C ambient temperature - hotter than any recorded Dubai summer day. Our active cooling system maintains safe TV operating temperature even in direct afternoon sun.

The system is specifically engineered for Gulf conditions: extreme heat, humidity, sand, and dust.

What does IP55 rating mean?

IP55 means:

  • 5 (Dust): Protected against dust ingress - not fully sealed but enough that dust won't affect operation
  • 5 (Water): Protected against low-pressure water jets from any direction - handles rain and splashes

This is the same protection level as outdoor security cameras and commercial signage.

Can I leave it outside in the rain?

Yes. The enclosure handles rain, including heavy Gulf storms. The double-layer labyrinth intake baffles and 75mm exhaust deflector prevent water ingress even in driving rain.

Note: While the enclosure is fully weatherproof, we recommend covered/shaded installation when possible - it runs cooler, looks better, and gives the best viewing experience.

What about sand and dust?

All air intake is filtered through a MERV 8 filter that catches sand and dust. The filter is easily accessible for cleaning - just slide out the filter drawer, rinse or replace, and slide back in.

Will pool chemicals or salt air damage it?

No. The enclosure uses marine-grade 6063-T5 aluminum with powder coating, and all exposed hardware is 316 stainless steel (marine grade). This is the same material spec used for beachfront installations.

What about recess/flush mounting?

Yes, we support recess installation for a flush, built-in look. This is ideal for new construction or renovations. The enclosure needs a 160mm deep cavity plus 60mm top gap for exhaust airflow.

Recess dimensions provided at time of order for your contractor to prepare the wall opening.

How does the TV go inside?

The enclosure has a hinged service door. You:

  1. Attach the VESA bracket to your TV on a table
  2. Open the enclosure door
  3. Hook the TV bracket onto the rail inside
  4. Close and secure the door

No need to hold the TV in place while threading bolts - it's a hook-and-hang system.

Sound & Audio

Can I hear the TV through the enclosure?

The glass front does reduce sound from the TV's built-in speakers. For casual viewing, it's usually fine - you'll hear dialogue and program audio. But for sports or movies outdoors, we strongly recommend external speakers.

Our recommendation: Rather than pretend this isn't an issue, we acknowledge it and recommend a proper solution. External Bluetooth speakers sound better outdoors anyway - even with a normal outdoor TV.
What speakers do you recommend?

Three options depending on budget and needs:

  • Budget (~AED 480): JBL Flip 7 - IP67 waterproof, portable, great for pool areas
  • Permanent install (~AED 750/pair): Polk Atrium 4 - wired outdoor speakers, mount under patio
  • Premium (~AED 1,650): Sonos Move 2 - smart speaker with auto-tuning, WiFi streaming

Ask about speaker bundles when ordering - we can source and include with your enclosure.

Is the enclosure noisy?

No. The cooling fans run at 24.6 dBA maximum - quieter than rustling leaves. During evening viewing when it's cooler, fans run at 30% speed producing only 12-14 dBA, which is essentially inaudible.

Our PWM-controlled fans adjust speed smoothly based on temperature - no noisy on/off cycling. They run quietly at low speed most of the time.

Maintenance

How do I clean the glass?

Wipe with a soft cloth and standard glass cleaner. The anti-reflective coating resists fingerprints and smudges.

How often should I change the filter?

Check monthly, clean or replace as needed. In dusty environments, you may need to rinse the filter every 2-4 weeks. The filter drawer slides out from the front - no tools needed.

Replacement filters are standard MERV 8 - available locally or we can supply.

What if my TV breaks?

Open the enclosure, remove the TV, and replace it with any TV of the same size. The enclosure is designed for easy TV swapping - you're not locked into one TV forever.

Bonus: Your replacement TV is covered by its own manufacturer warranty. With "outdoor TVs", you'd be buying a whole new expensive unit.
