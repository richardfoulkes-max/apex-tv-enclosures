Design Priorities

Thermal Performance is Critical

Must handle 55¬∞C ambient + solar load. This is why we need 3 high-static fans and proper airflow path - not just vents.

Serviceability Matters

Filters need cleaning, fans may need replacing. Hinged door means no glass removal for service.

IP55 Throughout

Every joint, every cable entry, every hinge must maintain the IP55 seal. This isn't just rain - it's garden hose cleaning.
