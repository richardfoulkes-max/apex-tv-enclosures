  • 55¬∞C ambient rating vs 50¬∞C
  • IR rejection glass reduces heat load
  • 140mm premium fans vs 80mm generic
  • 316 stainless hardware (coastal grade)

Weaknesses

  • No brand recognition yet
  • No established distribution

  1. Fast Track (1-2 weeks): Contact UAE fabricators (Al Maha, Precision Metal) for local prototype. Higher cost but faster turnaround. They can make the housing - you source fans/glass locally.
  2. Cost Optimized (4-6 weeks): Contact 2-3 Chinese pure OEM fabricators (Runze, Fortuna, Ningbo OEM). Send your spec as PDF, request 2-3 samples with all components.
  3. Hybrid Approach: Get aluminum housing fabricated in China, source Delta AFB1412HH-A fans and tempered glass locally in UAE, assemble yourself.
  4. Always send specification.html as PDF + the technical drawings
  5. Request sample unit before committing to production run
