Why GCC is Perfect for Premium Storage
  • Extreme sun: Plastic storage degrades in 2-3 years under UAE sun
  • High wealth: Villa owners spend $50K+ on outdoor furniture
  • Aesthetics matter: Pool areas are entertainment spaces, not utility zones
  • Year-round use: Pools used 10-12 months/year (not seasonal like EU/US)
  • Housekeeping culture: Staff maintain pool areas daily - need organized storage
Current Solutions (All Inadequate)
  • Plastic deck boxes: $100-300, fade/crack in 2-3 years, look cheap
  • Utility room: Inconvenient, not weather-resistant pathway
  • Indoor storage: Wet items, chlorine smell inside the house
  • No storage: Clutter around pool, trips over toys
  • Custom built: $5,000-15,000, 4-6 week lead time
