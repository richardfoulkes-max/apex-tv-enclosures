üõ°Ô∏è Objection Handling

‚ùì "It's expensive - I can just build a wall around the equipment"
Response: A basic block wall costs AED 2,000-4,000 and gives you no noise reduction, no access panels, no ventilation design, and no color matching. Then you still hear the pump and need to knock down the wall for major service. Our enclosure costs similar but solves all the problems properly - plus you can move it if you ever relocate equipment.
‚ùì "Underground pits keep everything hidden"
Response: Underground works in temperate climates. In Dubai, ground temperatures exceed 50¬∞C - that's hotter than equipment is designed to operate. Plus flooding is common, and every service call requires excavation. Our enclosure is shaded, cool, and opens in seconds.
‚ùì "Can't you just put some acoustic panels around it?"
Response: Acoustic panels need to fully enclose the equipment to work - any gap ruins the effect. And panels don't weather well outdoors. Our enclosure is a complete system: acoustic foam inside, weatherproof outside, designed airflow, and proper access. It's the difference between a proper solution and a workaround.
‚ùì "The equipment already has a warranty - why do I need protection?"
Response: Equipment warranties typically cover manufacturing defects, not environmental damage. UV degradation, sand infiltration, and heat stress aren't covered. Our enclosure protects against exactly those things, so your equipment actually lasts the full expected lifespan.
‚ùì "Will this affect my equipment warranty?"
Response: No - our enclosures are designed to meet or exceed manufacturer ventilation requirements. For heat pumps and chillers, our louvered design provides 50%+ open area - more than most manufacturers specify. We can provide documentation for warranty questions.
