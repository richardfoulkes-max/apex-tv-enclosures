Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to stock inventory?

No. We manufacture to order. You sell, collect payment, and we ship. Zero inventory risk.

What's the lead time?

4-6 weeks from order to delivery. Rush orders available for +15% (3 weeks).

Can I add my own margin for installation?

Absolutely. Most partners charge $300-500 for installation on top of the product price. That's 100% yours.

How do I handle warranty claims?

Contact us directly. We handle all warranty claims and replacements. 2-year warranty on all products.

Can I get exclusive territory?

Premier Partners can request exclusive territory protection. Subject to volume commitments.

What payment terms are available?

Authorized: Payment with order. Preferred: 50% deposit. Premier: Net-30 after credit approval.
