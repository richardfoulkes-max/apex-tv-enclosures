Deposit-First Sales

ZERO RISK

How It Works:

  • 50% deposit required to confirm order
  • Deposit covers full manufacturing cost + shipping
  • You never use your own capital for production orders
  • Balance due before/on delivery
  • Customer has skin in the game - no cancellations

Example: 75" enclosure sells for $2,000. Customer pays $1,000 deposit. Your cost is ~$600 landed. You're already $400 in profit before shipping anything.

Dual Supply Chain

GLOBAL REACH

Supply Strategy by Region:

  • Global (USA, Europe, Australia): Ship direct from China (KDM Steel) via sea/air freight
  • Middle East (UAE, Saudi): Manufacture locally for faster delivery, no import duty
  • Bulk orders (10+): Always ship from China for best margin
  • Rush orders: Premium pricing for local Middle East production

Why Enclosure Beats Outdoor TV

  • Use any TV: Samsung, Sony, TCL - your choice, not locked to one brand
  • Easy replacement: TV fails in 5 years? $800 for new one, not $10,000
  • Better protection: IP66 (Apex) vs IP55 (most outdoor TVs)
  • Latest tech: Upgrade to newest TV models anytime
  • Active cooling: Outdoor TVs have passive cooling - Apex has thermostat-controlled fans
  • Price as outdoor TVs drop: Your enclosure still protects the cheaper new TVs

Why We Always Win on Price

  • Indoor TVs drop faster: Competition is fiercer, tech advances faster
  • Enclosure cost is fixed: Aluminum and fans don't get cheaper like electronics
  • Math example: If Terrace drops to $5,000 and indoor TV drops to $600, Apex bundle = $1,600. Still 68% cheaper.
  • Replacement advantage: Their TV dies = buy new $5,000 outdoor TV. Our TV dies = $600 replacement.
