Design Language Analysis

These concept renders establish a distinctive visual identity for the APE-S storage line through consistent geometric treatment and premium material finishes.

Signature Design Elements

Chamfered Corners
  • 45° beveled edges on all vertical corners
  • Creates distinctive silhouette instantly recognizable as Apex
  • Softens industrial form into furniture-grade aesthetic
  • Consistent across all four product sizes
Two-Tone Color Strategy
  • Champagne/Sand tone for warm environments
  • Lunar Silver/Grey for modern spaces
  • Both work beautifully poolside
  • Contrasting lid/body adds depth
Copper/Bronze Hardware
  • Warm accent against cool aluminum
  • Minimal, bar-style handles
  • Premium feel without being ostentatious
  • Coordinates across product range
Manufacturing Considerations
  • Chamfered corners add fabrication complexity
  • May require CNC routing or specialized welding
  • Worth the premium positioning tradeoff
  • Consider simplified version for value tier

Proposed Product Range

Tall Cabinet

APE-S-TC

Upright wardrobe-style storage with internal shelving. Ideal for pool noodles, cleaning equipment, and vertical items.

  • Double-door front access
  • Adjustable internal shelves
  • Interior LED lighting
  • Estimated: 600 × 500 × 1400mm

Large Chest

APE-S-LC

Horizontal blanket-box style for cushions, towels, and bulky items. Gas-strut assisted lid.

  • Full-width hinged top
  • STABILUS soft-close struts
  • Drainage holes for wet items
  • Estimated: 1200 × 600 × 600mm

Medium Cube

APE-S-MC

Compact top-opening cube. Perfect as side table that doubles as storage.

  • Top-hinged lid
  • Can function as seat/table
  • Ideal for pool toys, goggles
  • Estimated: 500 × 500 × 500mm

Small Cabinet

APE-S-SC

Double-door front access cabinet. Lockable for pool chemicals or valuables.

  • Twin doors with push-to-open
  • Optional internal shelf
  • Key lock standard
  • Estimated: 600 × 500 × 600mm

Preliminary Specifications

Attribute Specification
Material 5052-H32 Aluminum, 2mm panels, 3mm frame
Corner Treatment 45° chamfer on vertical corners (signature detail)
Corner Radius 12mm on horizontal edges (per Apex design language)
Hardware Copper/bronze anodized handles, 316 SS internals
Hinges Concealed European (Blum) with soft-close
Gas Struts STABILUS LIFT-O-MAT (chests only)
Finish Options Champagne Sand, Lunar Silver, Graphite, Cloud White
Weather Rating IP44 (splash resistant, not submersible)
Ventilation Discreet drainage slots in base
Interior Optional LED lighting with door switch
