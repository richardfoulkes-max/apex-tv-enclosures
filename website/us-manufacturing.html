1. Why US Manufacturing is Required

Critical Tariff Barrier: As of June 2025, the US imposes a 50% Section 232 tariff on aluminum products from UAE. This makes UAE-manufactured enclosures uncompetitive in the US market.

Tariff Impact Analysis

Cost Component UAE to US US Domestic
Manufacturing (FOB) $870 $1,100 - $1,400
Shipping $50 $50 - $100
50% Tariff (Section 232) $435 $0
Landed Cost ~$1,355 ~$1,150 - $1,500
Key Insight: US manufacturing is cost-competitive after tariffs, offers faster lead times (2-4 weeks vs 6-8 weeks), and can match Eurotech quality with AAMA 2604 (1000hr salt spray - standard for exterior architectural work).

4. Powder Coating Quality Standards

Specify AAMA 2604 coating standard - this is the industry standard for exterior architectural aluminum and any qualified fabricator can meet it.

Standard Salt Spray Rating Use Case Notes
AAMA 2603 500 hours Basic indoor/outdoor Entry level, not recommended
AAMA 2604 1000 hours Standard exterior Recommended - industry standard
AAMA 2605 4000 hours Coastal / Marine High-rise facades - overkill for enclosures
Eurotech (reference) 2000 hours Marine grade Current UAE supplier standard
Note: Established fabricators like Bull Metal and Accurate Metal Fab have in-house powder coating lines or trusted coating partners. Simply specify "AAMA 2604 minimum" in your RFQ - they'll handle the rest.
