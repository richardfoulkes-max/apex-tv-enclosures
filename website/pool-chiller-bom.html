6. APE-HC vs APE-P Cost Comparison

Heater/Chiller enclosures are generally less expensive than Pump enclosures due to simpler construction (no acoustic foam, simpler ventilation).

Feature APE-P (Pump) APE-HC (Heater/Chiller)
Panel construction Sealed + 25mm acoustic foam Louvered (no foam)
Acoustic foam cost ~$35-45 $0
Panel fabrication More complex (sealed joints) Simpler (louver press)
Gaskets/seals Required (IP55) Minimal (IP44)
Typical BOM difference Baseline ~15-20% lower
