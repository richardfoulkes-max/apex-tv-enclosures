V2 Acoustic Design Overview

The APE-HC V2 uses a three-component architecture for 15-25 dB noise reduction while maintaining full airflow capacity:

Base Enclosure

Open-frame top design with aluminum frame, back panel, floor panel, and access door. Sits on anti-vibration mounts.

Intake Modules (x2)

Side-mounted baffled intake with 90° air turn. Weather louvers + mineral wool + perforated liner.

Discharge Hood

Removable acoustic hood with solid ceiling, insulated walls, and side acoustic louvers. Crane-removable for service.

Acoustic Treatment

Mineral wool absorption (48 kg/m³), butyl damping mat on panels, optional MLV on door for additional mass.

V2 Acoustic vs V1 Louvered Design

The V2 acoustic design adds significant noise reduction capability at higher cost and weight:

Feature V1 (Simple Louvered) V2 (Acoustic)
Noise Reduction ~10 dB (weather louvers only) 15-25 dB (full acoustic treatment)
Intake Design Direct louvers (straight-through) 90° baffled turn with absorption
Exhaust Design Open top with weather louvers Removable discharge hood with acoustic outlets
Panel Treatment None Butyl damping mat (CLD)
Vibration Isolation Basic feet Rubber-metal anti-vib mounts
Service Access Front door only Front door + removable hood (crane lift)
BOM Cost (Medium) ~$185 ~$360 (+95%)
Weight (Medium) ~45kg ~110kg (+144%)
When to use V2: Noise-sensitive applications (residential near bedrooms/living areas, hospitality, premium installations). The V2 design targets near-inaudible operation at property boundaries.
