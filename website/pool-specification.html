1.2 Design Philosophy

Why Fixed Sizes?

Three standard sizes cover 95% of residential pool equipment configurations. Made-to-order manufacturing by Eurotech allows for quality control and competitive pricing without complex modular connections.

Why Acoustic?

Pool pumps generate 65-80 dB at 1m - louder than a vacuum cleaner. In villa communities, noise complaints are common. Acoustic lining differentiates from DIY solutions and commands premium pricing.

1.3 Target Market

  • Primary: UAE/GCC luxury villa owners (Emirates Hills, Palm Jumeirah, Al Barari)
  • Secondary: Hotel/resort pool areas, community pool managers
  • Channel: Pool contractors (Medley Pool, Astralux), landscape architects

2.4 Selecting the Right Size

Measure Your Equipment

  1. Measure width, depth, height of each piece
  2. Add 50mm clearance on all sides
  3. Account for plumbing connections
  4. Choose the size with sufficient internal space

When in Doubt, Go Larger

Extra space improves ventilation and makes servicing easier. The price difference between sizes is minimal compared to equipment access benefits.

3.2 Rain-Proof Acoustic Baffle Design

All ventilation openings use downward-angled louvers that block rain while maintaining airflow and acoustic performance. Unlike open-gap designs (which leak in rain), our louvered system is fully rain-proof.

üåßÔ∏è
Rain-Proof Guarantee
Louvered vents angled at 30-45¬∞ shed water externally. No rain enters the enclosure - even in UAE thunderstorms or when cleaning with a garden hose.

Intake Louvers (Bottom/Sides)

  • Louvered design (30¬∞ downward angle)
  • Double-layer with 25mm stagger
  • Drip edges on each louver blade
  • Foam-lined internal surfaces
  • Effective open area: 40%
  • Sound reduction: 15-20 dB
  • Rain ingress: Zero

Exhaust Louvers (Top)

  • Louvered design (45¬∞ downward angle)
  • 75mm deep louver blades
  • Foam strips on louver backs
  • Overlapping design blocks vertical rain
  • Sound reduction: 10-15 dB
  • Rain ingress: Zero
