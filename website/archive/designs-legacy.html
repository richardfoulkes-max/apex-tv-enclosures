Legacy Designs - ATE Series (v1.0-v2.0)

Archived designs - Rear-exhaust airflow architecture

ARCHIVED - DO NOT USE FOR MANUFACTURING
These designs show the legacy v1.0-v2.0 rear-exhaust architecture. The current design is v3.0 Front-Breathing.

Key differences from v3.0:
  • v2.0: 115mm depth vs v3.0: 120mm depth
  • v2.0: 20mm bezel vs v3.0: 35mm bezel with airflow slots
  • v2.0: 4×80mm rear-mounted fans vs v3.0: 4×60mm top-bezel fans
  • v2.0: Rear exhaust (blocks wall-mount) vs v3.0: Front-breathing (universal mount)
