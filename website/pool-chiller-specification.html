1.2 Why Open Frame Top + Acoustic Hood?

The Problem

Heat pump fans discharge vertically. A solid roof would block airflow ‚Üí overheating, create backpressure ‚Üí reduced efficiency, and trap heat ‚Üí component damage.

The Solution

Open aluminum frame (structural only) with a removable acoustic discharge hood that redirects exhaust air sideways through large-area acoustic outlets while blocking direct line-of-sight from fan to exterior.

4.4 Panel Damping (CLD)

Constrained-Layer Damping reduces panel resonance by 10-15 dB:

Application

  • Material: Butyl-based damping mat (2-3mm thick)
  • Coverage: 60-80% of each large panel
  • Location: Interior surface of all panels >300mm span
  • Pattern: Strips or patches, avoiding airflow paths

Optional MLV

  • Material: 1-2 lb/ft¬≤ Mass-Loaded Vinyl
  • Application: Back of door panel, solid sections only
  • NOT on louvers or airflow paths
  • Adds additional mass for low-frequency attenuation

6 Assembly & Installation

6.1 Base Enclosure Assembly

  1. Frame Assembly: Bolt corner posts to base frame using 316 SS M8 bolts. Verify squareness (diagonal measurement ¬±2mm).
  2. Panel Installation: Install back panel first (fixed), then side intake modules (bolted, removable), then door with hinges and compression latches.
  3. CLD Application: Apply butyl damping mat to interior of all panels.
  4. Top Frame: Install top frame (structural members only). Top frame supports discharge hood weight. Include lifting eyes for hood removal.

6.2 Intake Module Assembly

  1. Apply 25mm mineral wool to vertical baffle
  2. Cover with perforated aluminum liner
  3. Install coarse debris screen (removable)
  4. Attach weather louver to exterior
  5. Seal edges with silicone or gasket

6.3 Discharge Hood Assembly

  1. Structure: Assemble hood frame, install ceiling panel (solid)
  2. Acoustic Lining: Line all interior surfaces with 50mm mineral wool, cover with perforated aluminum liner, secure with rivets or screws
  3. Outlets: Install acoustic louvers on designated sides (verify free-area matches design)
  4. Mounting: Hood sits on top frame, secure with toggle clamps (4 minimum), ensure 20mm clearance from fan discharge

6.4 Equipment Installation

  1. Place anti-vibration mounts on enclosure floor
  2. Position heat pump on mounts
  3. Level using mount adjustment
  4. Connect pipework with flexible sections
  5. Route electrical through sealed gland (IP66)
‚ö°
Electrical Safety
All electrical work must be performed by licensed electrician. Use weatherproof cable glands (IP66). Equipment disconnect must be accessible without opening enclosure.
