Which Product for Which Use Case?

Choose Apex ATE Series When:

  • Installation has ANY shade (covered patio, pergola, alcove)
  • Budget is a primary concern
  • 4K resolution and smart TV features are desired
  • TV upgrades may be needed in future
  • Coastal or high-humidity location (aluminum resists corrosion)
  • Noise-sensitive environment (pool area near bedrooms)
  • Local service/repair is important

Choose Aqualite AQLH Series When:

  • Installation is in DIRECT sunlight with no shade
  • Budget is not a concern (commercial/hospitality)
  • High-impact environment (public areas, sports bars)
  • RS-232 automation integration required
  • EU/UK warranty support is acceptable
Key Insight: For most Gulf residential installations (covered patios, pool areas with shade structures), Apex delivers superior value. Aqualite only makes sense for unshaded commercial signage where maximum brightness is critical.
