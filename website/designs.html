ATE-75: Outdoor TV Enclosure (75") - Integrated Bezel Design v5.3.0 IP55

Design Intent: IP55 weatherproof enclosure for commercial 75" TVs in Gulf region outdoor environments. v5.3.0 eliminates the protruding exhaust deflector in favor of integrated 80mm top/bottom bezels with recessed ventilation slots. Clean "picture frame" aesthetic with flush front face. Ducted rear-plenum thermal management with perforated diffuser plate provides direct cooling of TV heat sinks. Compatible with both wall-mount AND recessed installations.

Air enters through bottom bezel slots, passes through gasketed MERV 8 filter drawer, flows through perforated diffuser plate (8mm holes, 40% open) into 30mm rear plenum, directly cooling TV heat sinks, then exhausts via 3× Delta AFB1412HH-A fans (165-195 CFM) through top bezel slots.
