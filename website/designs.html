Engineering Designs - ATE Series (v5.2.1)

CAD-grade specifications for outdoor TV enclosures - Recess-Compatible Ducted Plenum Architecture

ATE-75: Outdoor TV Enclosure (75") - Recess-Compatible v5.2.1

Design Intent: IP54 weatherproof enclosure for commercial 75" TVs in Gulf region outdoor environments. Ducted rear-plenum thermal management with perforated diffuser plate provides direct cooling of TV heat sinks. Compatible with both wall-mount AND recessed installations.

Thermal Margin: At 55°C ambient + 550W load + 210-250 CFM forced cooling through 30mm rear plenum with diffuser plate, thermal margin is 80-115% wall-mounted or 30-50% recessed. Perforated diffuser plate ensures even airflow distribution across TV heat sinks. Passed ChatGPT engineering validation (PASS FOR PROTOTYPE).
IP54 Rating: Continuous EPDM gasket seal, IP68 cable glands, labyrinth intake louvers (angled down 45°), exhaust louvers (angled up), hinged service door with compression seal. IP54 is realistic with active ventilation - dust protected + splash resistant.
