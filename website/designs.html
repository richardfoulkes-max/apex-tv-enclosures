ATE-75: Outdoor TV Enclosure (75") - Front-Breathing v3.0

Design Intent: IP66 weatherproof enclosure for commercial 75" TVs in Gulf region outdoor environments. Front-breathing thermal management enables wall-mount, recessed, and flush installations.

Thermal Margin: At 55°C ambient + 350W load + 100 CFM cooling (with natural convection assist from bottom-to-rear airflow), internal delta-T is approximately 12-18°C above ambient. Internal temperature: 67-73°C air, but TV surface receives direct airflow keeping display under 50°C. Thermally validated with convection-assisted design. Recommend white powder coat to reduce solar absorption by ~40%.
IP66 Validation: Continuous EPDM gasket seal, IP66 cable glands, labyrinth intake grilles with internal baffles, rain cowl exhaust with 40mm overhang. Design meets IEC 60529 requirements for dust-tight + protection against powerful water jets.
