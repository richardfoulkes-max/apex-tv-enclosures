Thermal Margin: At 55°C ambient + 350W load + 120 CFM cooling, internal delta-T is approximately 10-15°C above ambient. Internal temperature: 65-70°C air, but TV surface receives direct airflow keeping display under 50°C. Marginal but acceptable. Recommend white powder coat to reduce solar absorption by ~40%.
IP66 Validation: Continuous EPDM gasket seal, IP66 cable glands, labyrinth intake grilles with internal baffles, rain cowl exhaust with 40mm overhang. Design meets IEC 60529 requirements for dust-tight + protection against powerful water jets.
