Engineering Designs - ATE Series (v5.2.2 IP55)

CAD-grade specifications for outdoor TV enclosures - Recess-Compatible Ducted Plenum Architecture

ATE-75: Outdoor TV Enclosure (75") - Recess-Compatible v5.2.2 IP55

Design Intent: IP55 weatherproof enclosure for commercial 75" TVs in Gulf region outdoor environments. IP55 ducted rear-plenum thermal management with double-layer labyrinth intake, 75mm exhaust deflector, and perforated diffuser plate provides direct cooling of TV heat sinks while protecting against low-pressure water jets (garden hose safe). Compatible with both wall-mount AND recessed installations.

Recess-compatible: 1820 × 1130 × 215mm cavity with 60mm top gap prevents exhaust recirculation. IP55: Garden hose cleanable.
