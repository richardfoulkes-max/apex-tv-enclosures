TV Compatibility Guide

Which TVs work best with outdoor enclosures - and which to avoid

RECOMMENDED

Best Choice

  • Samsung QLED/LED (Crystal UHD)
  • Sony Bravia LED
  • TCL QLED (C series)
  • Hisense ULED
USE WITH CAUTION

May Work

  • LG NanoCell (LED only)
  • Vizio (lower heat tolerance)
  • Budget brands (check depth)
AVOID

Not Recommended

  • ANY OLED TV (LG, Sony, Samsung)
  • TVs deeper than 70mm
  • Curved screens

DO NOT USE OLED TVs IN OUTDOOR ENCLOSURES

  • Heat sensitivity: OLED panels are extremely heat-sensitive. ~60,000 LG OLED TVs affected by overheating issues.
  • Direct sunlight damage: LG warns sunlight can cause "discoloration or deterioration of the liquid crystal"
  • Burn-in risk: Static images (logos, channel bugs) cause permanent damage faster in heat
  • Power board failures: Known issues with current-controlling components overheating
  • This includes: LG OLED (C/G series), Sony OLED (A series), Samsung QD-OLED (S95 series)

TV Settings for Outdoor Use

Use "Standard" or "Movie" mode
"Vivid" generates more heat
Reduce backlight during hottest hours
Lower brightness = less heat generated
Disable auto-brightness if overheating
Some TVs throttle in heat - manual control is better
Enable power saving features
Reduces heat when idle
Set sleep timer
Auto-off prevents unnecessary heat buildup
