1. Why Consider European Manufacturing?

Good News: Unlike the US (50% tariff), the EU only charges ~6% duty on UAE aluminum products. Eurotech remains viable for EU markets.

However, local European manufacturing offers significant advantages for UK/EU customers:

Factor UAE (Eurotech) European Local
Delivery Time 3-4 weeks (sea freight) 1-2 weeks (road)
Returns/Warranty Complex international shipping Simple domestic return
Communication Time zone differences Same/similar timezone
Sample/Prototype 4-6 weeks 1-2 weeks
Carbon Footprint Long-haul shipping Lower emissions
Unit Cost (est.) ~$950-1,000 landed ~$1,200-1,500
Recommendation: For UK/EU sales, European manufacturing is worth the extra cost for better customer experience. Returns and warranty issues are much simpler with local production.

4. European Coating Standards (Qualicoat)

Europe uses Qualicoat instead of AAMA. Specify Qualicoat Class 2 for outdoor enclosures - equivalent to AAMA 2604.

Standard Durability Use Case Notes
Qualicoat Class 1 1 year Florida test Basic exterior Entry level
Qualicoat Class 2 50% more durable than Class 1 Standard exterior Recommended - "Super Durable"
Qualicoat Class 3 Extreme durability Hot climates, extreme UV Overkill for enclosures
Qualicoat Seaside Enhanced pretreatment Within 5km of coast Optional add-on for coastal
RFQ Tip: Specify "Qualicoat Class 2 minimum" in your RFQs. Most European architectural fabricators are familiar with this standard and either hold Qualicoat licenses or use Qualicoat-approved coating suppliers.

Alternative Standards

  • GSB: German/Austrian/Swiss standard - equivalent quality to Qualicoat
  • BS EN 12206-1:2021: British/European standard aligned with Qualicoat
  • Qualanod: For anodized aluminum (different process)

7. Notes for Scott (UK/EU Sales)

UK Market

Recommended Source: UK manufacturer (Universal Fab or Profab)

Est. Landed Cost: ~$1,350-1,650

Lead Time: 1-2 weeks

Key Benefit: Easy returns, no import paperwork for customer

Selling Point: "Made in Britain" - premium positioning

EU Market

Recommended Source: Delvalle (Spain) or Animus (Poland)

Est. Landed Cost: ~$1,150-1,500

Lead Time: 1-2 weeks

Key Benefit: No UK-EU customs, EU warranty compliance

Selling Point: "Made in EU" - quality + local support

Alternative: Eurotech for UK/EU

If price is the priority, Eurotech remains viable:

  • ~6% duty to UK/EU (not 50% like US)
  • Landed cost ~$1,000-1,050 (cheaper than local)
  • Trade-off: Longer delivery (3-4 weeks), complex returns
  • Best for: Price-sensitive customers willing to wait
Pricing Strategy: Offer both options - "Standard" (Eurotech, lower price, 3-4 week delivery) vs "Premium" (Local, higher price, 1-2 week delivery + easy returns). Let customer choose.
