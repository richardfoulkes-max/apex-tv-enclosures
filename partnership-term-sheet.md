## 4. Vesting Schedule

Partner's 50% equity vests over 24 months based on commitment and performance:

| Milestone | Equity Earned | Cumulative |
|-----------|---------------|------------|
| Day 1: Full commitment begins | 10% | 10% |
| First AED 100,000 in sales | 10% | 20% |
| First AED 300,000 in sales | 10% | 30% |
| First AED 500,000 in sales | 10% | 40% |
| 24 months active + AED 750,000 total | 10% | 50% |

**If Partner leaves or stops actively working:**
- Retains only vested equity at that date
- Unvested equity returns to RF

## 5. Roles & Decisions

| Area | Lead |
|------|------|

| Finance, Legal, Admin | Joint |
| Major decisions (>AED 50,000 spend) | Both must agree |

---

## 6. Money

### Before Profitability
- No salaries
- Business expenses covered by revenue (or RF if needed)
- Both partners work "at risk"

### After Profitability
- Equal profit distributions (50/50)
- Reinvestment decisions made jointly
- Optional: Small equal monthly draws once sustainable

---

## 7. Exit & Protection

- **Non-compete:** If either partner exits, they cannot start or join a competing outdoor enclosure business in the GCC for 2 years
- **IP:** All product designs, systems, and brand assets belong to the company (not individuals)
- **Buyout:** If one partner wants out, the other has first right to buy their shares at fair market value
- **Deadlock:** If we can't agree on major decisions, we'll use a mutually agreed mediator

## 9. The Spirit of This Partnership

We're building this together. Richard built the product and systems. Partner will build the sales and customer base. Neither succeeds without the other.

50/50 means equal ownership, equal say, equal upside. The vesting simply ensures we're both committed for the long haul.
