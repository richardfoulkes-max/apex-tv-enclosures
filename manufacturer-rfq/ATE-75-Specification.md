# ATE-75 Outdoor TV Enclosure - Manufacturing Specification

## Product Overview
Weather-resistant enclosure for 75" commercial displays, featuring front-breathing thermal management for outdoor deployment.

---

## External Dimensions
| Dimension | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Width | 1760 mm |
| Height | 1040 mm |
| Depth | 120 mm |

---

## Construction

### Material
- **Body**: 3mm aluminum sheet (powder-coated or anodized)
- **Front Glass**: 6mm tempered glass, anti-reflective coating preferred

### Wall Thickness
- All walls: 3mm
- Bezel frame width: 35mm (all sides)

---

## Front Panel Features

### Viewing Area (Glass Opening)
- Width: 1690 mm (1760 - 35×2)
- Height: 970 mm (1040 - 35×2)

### Bottom Intake Slot
- Width: 1700 mm
- Height: 15 mm
- Position: Centered horizontally, 10mm from bottom edge

### Top Exhaust Slot
- Width: 1700 mm
- Height: 20 mm
- Position: Centered horizontally, 7.5mm from top edge

### Fan Mounting Holes (4×)
- Diameter: 60 mm each
- Position: In top bezel, evenly spaced across 1700mm
- Depth: 15mm recessed
- X positions from center: -510mm, -170mm, +170mm, +510mm

---

## Rear Panel Features

### VESA Mount Pattern
- Pattern: 600 × 400 mm (VESA MIS-F)
- Hole size: M8 (8mm diameter)
- Position: Centered on rear panel
- Hole positions from center:
  - (-300, -200), (+300, -200)
  - (-300, +200), (+300, +200)

### Cable Entry Holes (2×)
- Diameter: 30 mm
- Position: Near bottom of rear panel
- X positions from center: -200mm, +200mm
- Y position: 100mm from bottom edge

---

## Glass Panel
| Dimension | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Width | 1686 mm |
| Height | 966 mm |
| Thickness | 6 mm |
| Type | Tempered, safety glass |
| Mounting | Recessed into bezel, sealed |

---

## Thermal Design
- **Airflow**: Passive convection + forced exhaust
- **Intake**: Bottom slot (cool air enters)
- **Exhaust**: Top slot + 4× 60mm fans
- **Air gap**: 25mm between glass and TV

---

## Sealing Requirements
- IP55 rating minimum (dust protected, water jet resistant)
- Gasket seal around glass panel
- Cable entry grommets required
