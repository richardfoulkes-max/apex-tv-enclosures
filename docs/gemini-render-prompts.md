## Storage Bench (APE-S-SB)

```

- Dimensions: approximately 1500mm wide × 450mm deep × 450mm height (seat height)

The bench should look sturdy enough to sit on while maintaining the elegant, architectural feel of the reference product family. Show the interior cavity when lid is open - simple clean interior with drainage holes visible in base.

Style: Premium outdoor furniture, minimalist, architectural. Match the exact design language, materials, and lighting of the attached reference renders.
```
