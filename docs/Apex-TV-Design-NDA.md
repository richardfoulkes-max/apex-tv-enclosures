## Purpose

The parties wish to explore a potential business relationship regarding the manufacture of outdoor TV enclosures. This agreement protects confidential design information shared during this evaluation.

---

## 1. Confidential Information

"Confidential Information" means:
- Product designs, drawings, and specifications
- Manufacturing methods and processes
- Technical data and engineering details
- Pricing and business terms

## 2. Obligations

The Receiving Party agrees to:

1. **Not disclose** the Confidential Information to any third party without prior written consent
2. **Not use** the Confidential Information to design, manufacture, or sell competing products
3. **Not share** the Confidential Information with competitors of the Disclosing Party
4. **Protect** the Confidential Information with reasonable care (at least the same care used for their own confidential information)

## 3. Exclusions

This Agreement does not apply to information that:
- Is already publicly known
- Was already known to the Receiving Party before disclosure
- Is independently developed without using Confidential Information
- Is required to be disclosed by law

## 4. Return of Materials

Upon request or termination of discussions, the Receiving Party will return or destroy all Confidential Information and any copies.

## 5. Term

This Agreement remains in effect for **3 years** from the date of signing.
