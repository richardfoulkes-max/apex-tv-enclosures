### Why 60mm Top Gap?

- 30mm was insufficient: hot exhaust hit cavity ceiling and recirculated
- 60mm + deflector: exhaust directed forward, exits cavity cleanly
- Prevents thermal bubble formation above enclosure
- Only 31mm taller cavity than competitor (1130 vs 1099)

## 3. PLENUM FLOW DISTRIBUTION (NEW)
================================================================================

### Problem Addressed
30mm × 1680mm plenum creates dead zones without flow distribution.

### Solution: Perforated Diffuser Plate

| Parameter | Specification |
|-----------|---------------|
| Location | Bottom of rear plenum, horizontal |
| Material | 1.5mm 5052-H32 Aluminum |
| Hole pattern | 8mm diameter holes |
| Open area | 40% |
| Purpose | Equalizes pressure across full plenum width |

### How It Works

```
BEFORE (v5.2):                    AFTER (v5.2.1):

Air enters bottom,                Air enters bottom,
takes path of least               hits diffuser plate,
resistance → dead zones           pressure equalizes →
                                  uniform distribution
     ↑   ↑                             ↑ ↑ ↑ ↑ ↑ ↑
     │   │                        ═════════════════════
     │   └── most air            (perforated diffuser)
     └────── little air                ↑ ↑ ↑ ↑ ↑ ↑
```

### Alternative Considered
Vertical baffles with cross-bleeds - more complex, chose diffuser for simplicity.

## 4. THERMAL MANAGEMENT
================================================================================

### Fan Specification (LOCKED MODEL)

| Parameter | Specification |
|-----------|---------------|
| Quantity | 3× (all active) |
| **Model** | **Delta AFB1412HH-A** |
| Size | 140mm × 140mm × 25mm |
| Airflow | 83 CFM each |
| Static Pressure | 4.8 mm H₂O |
| RPM Range | 500-3000 |
| Voltage | 12V DC |
| Rating | IP55 |
| **System Total** | **~210-250 CFM** |

### Why Delta AFB1412HH-A?
- High static pressure (4.8 mm H₂O) overcomes filter + plenum restriction
- IP55 rated for outdoor use
- Industrial reliability
- Consistent availability
- No model mixing = predictable system curve

### Thermal Performance (Updated with locked fan)

| Condition | Heat Load | Required | Delivered | Margin |
|-----------|-----------|----------|-----------|--------|
| Full sun (55°C) | 550W | 116 CFM | 210-250 | **+80-115%** |
| Recess full sun | 550W | 116 CFM | 150-175* | **+30-50%** |
| Shaded pergola | 350W | 60 CFM | 210-250 | **+250%** |

*Recess assumes 70% efficiency due to turns/restriction

### Fan Control

| Internal Temp | Fan Speed |
|---------------|-----------|
| <35°C | Off |
| 35-45°C | 30% PWM |
| 45-55°C | 50% PWM |
| 55-65°C | 75% PWM |
| >65°C | 100% PWM |

**Fail-safe:** 100% if sensor fails open

### Compression Control

| Feature | Specification |

================================================================================

| Feature | Specification |
|---------|---------------|
| Post-shutdown fan | 10% PWM for 30 minutes after TV off |
| ePTFE breather | Gore-Tex membrane on rear panel |
| Drain slots | 3× 5mm at bottom of plenum |
| Glass coating | Hydrophilic interior (anti-fog) |

### Post-Shutdown Logic

```
TV power-off detected →
  Fan duty = 10% (nearly silent)
  Timer = 30 minutes
  After 30 min → Fan duty = 0%

Purpose: Clear warm humid air before enclosure cools
         Prevents dew point condensation on glass/TV
```

## 9. VALIDATION QUESTIONS
================================================================================

Please confirm:

1. **Diffuser plate:** Does perforated plate (8mm holes, 40% open) adequately
   address plenum dead zones? Or should we use vertical baffles instead?

2. **Exhaust deflector + 60mm gap:** Is this sufficient to prevent recirculation
   in recess installs? Should the deflector angle be steeper (60°)?

3. **4 latches + compression stops:** Does this adequately address IP54 seal
   consistency over 1650mm span?

   curve adequate for filter + plenum + recess restriction?

5. **Recess thermal margin:** At 70% efficiency, 150-175 CFM vs 116 required
   gives 30-50% margin. Is this sufficient for Gulf worst-case?
